Destination: Tamil Nadu
Tamil Nadu is a southern Indian state known for temples, classical arts, beaches, hill stations, and ancient heritage.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Chennai:
A coastal city known for temples, beaches, music, and culture.

Madurai:
Famous for the Meenakshi Amman Temple and rich heritage.

Ooty:
A hill station known for tea gardens, lakes, and pleasant weather.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Idli and dosa
Pongal
Chettinad chicken
Filter coffee
Transportation:

Chennai International Airport is the main gateway.
Local transport includes buses, trains, taxis, metro, and rental cars.

Travel tips:

Dress respectfully while visiting temples.
Carry cotton clothes during summer.
Explore local festivals and cultural events.
