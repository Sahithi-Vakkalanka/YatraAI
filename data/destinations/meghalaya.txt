Destination: Meghalaya
Meghalaya is a northeastern Indian state known for waterfalls, caves, hills, and beautiful landscapes.

Best time to visit:
October to April.

Popular destinations:

Shillong:
Known as the "Scotland of the East" for its hills and pleasant climate.

Cherrapunji:
Famous for waterfalls, caves, and heavy rainfall.

Mawlynnong:
Known as one of Asia’s cleanest villages.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Jadoh
Dohneiihong
Nakham bitchi
Tungrymbai
Transportation:

Shillong Airport is the main airport.
Guwahati Airport is also commonly used.
Local transport includes taxis and shared vehicles.

Travel tips:

Carry rain protection.
Wear comfortable shoes for caves and waterfalls.
Avoid littering to protect natural areas.
