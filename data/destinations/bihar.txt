Destination: Bihar
Bihar is an eastern Indian state known for ancient history, Buddhist heritage, and religious sites.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Bodh Gaya:
A major Buddhist pilgrimage site associated with enlightenment of Buddha.

Nalanda:
Known for the ancient Nalanda University ruins.

Rajgir:
Famous for hills, hot springs, and historical sites.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Litti chokha
Sattu paratha
Khaja
Thekua
Transportation:

Jay Prakash Narayan International Airport in Patna is the main gateway.
Local transport includes buses, taxis, and auto-rickshaws.

Travel tips:

Winter is ideal for sightseeing.
Dress modestly while visiting religious places.
Carry water while exploring historical sites.
