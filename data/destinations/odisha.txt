Destination: Odisha
Odisha is an eastern Indian state known for temples, beaches, classical dance, wildlife, and cultural heritage.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Puri:
Famous for beaches and the Jagannath Temple.

Konark:
Known for the Sun Temple and ancient architecture.

Bhubaneswar:
Known as the "Temple City of India" with many historic temples.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Dalma
Pakhala bhata
Chhena poda
Macha ghanta
Transportation:

Biju Patnaik International Airport in Bhubaneswar is the main gateway.
Local transport includes buses, taxis, auto-rickshaws, and rental cars.

Travel tips:

Visit temples with proper dress.
Avoid coastal travel during cyclone warnings.
Try traditional Odia cuisine.
