Destination: Jharkhand
Jharkhand is an eastern Indian state known for forests, waterfalls, tribal culture, and mineral resources.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Ranchi:
Known as the "City of Waterfalls" with scenic attractions.

Netarhat:
A hill station famous for sunrise views and forests.

Deoghar:
A major pilgrimage destination known for temples.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Dhuska
Rugra
Chilka roti
Thekua
Transportation:

Birsa Munda Airport in Ranchi is the main gateway.
Local transport includes buses, taxis, and auto-rickshaws.

Travel tips:

Carry comfortable footwear for waterfalls and forests.
Visit tribal areas respectfully.
Winter is ideal for outdoor activities.
