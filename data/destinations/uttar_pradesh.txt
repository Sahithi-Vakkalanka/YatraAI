Destination: Uttar Pradesh
Uttar Pradesh is a northern Indian state known for spiritual sites, history, monuments, and cultural traditions.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Agra:
Famous for the Taj Mahal and Mughal heritage.

Varanasi:
One of the oldest living cities in the world, known for temples and river ghats.

Ayodhya:
A historic religious city known for temples and cultural importance.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Awadhi biryani
Tunday kebab
Kachori sabzi
Petha
Transportation:

Chaudhary Charan Singh International Airport in Lucknow and Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport in Varanasi are major gateways.
Local transport includes trains, buses, taxis, and auto-rickshaws.

Travel tips:

Visit monuments early to avoid crowds.
Respect religious customs.
Carry comfortable footwear for sightseeing.
