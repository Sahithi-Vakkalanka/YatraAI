Destination: Himachal Pradesh
Himachal Pradesh is a Himalayan state known for snow-covered mountains, valleys, temples, and adventure activities.

Best time to visit:
March to June and October to February.

Popular destinations:

Manali:
A popular hill station known for snow activities, trekking, and scenic views.

Shimla:
Famous for colonial architecture, mountains, and pleasant weather.

Dharamshala:
Known for monasteries, Tibetan culture, and mountain landscapes.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Dham
Siddu
Chana madra
Thukpa
Transportation:

Kangra Airport and Chandigarh Airport are major gateways.
Local transport includes buses, taxis, and rental cars.

Travel tips:

Carry warm clothes during winter.
Check weather conditions before mountain travel.
Drive carefully on hilly roads.
