Destination: Telangana
Telangana is a southern Indian state known for history, monuments, cuisine, and modern cities.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Hyderabad:
Known for historic monuments, technology, and famous cuisine.

Warangal:
Famous for forts, temples, and ancient architecture.

Nagarjuna Sagar:
Known for a large dam, scenic views, and historical sites.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Hyderabadi biryani
Haleem
Sarva pindi
Double ka meetha
Transportation:

Rajiv Gandhi International Airport in Hyderabad is the main gateway.
Local transport includes metro, buses, taxis, and rental cars.

Travel tips:

Carry water during summer.
Visit monuments early in the day.
Try traditional Hyderabadi food.
