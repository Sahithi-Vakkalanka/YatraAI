Destination: Chhattisgarh
Chhattisgarh is a central Indian state known for waterfalls, forests, tribal culture, and ancient temples.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Chitrakote Falls:
Known as the "Niagara Falls of India" for its wide waterfall.

Bastar:
Famous for tribal culture, handicrafts, and forests.

Kanger Valley National Park:
Known for caves, wildlife, and biodiversity.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Fara
Chila
Aamat
Bafauri
Transportation:

Swami Vivekananda Airport in Raipur is the main gateway.
Local transport includes buses, taxis, and rental vehicles.

Travel tips:

Carry comfortable footwear for nature trails.
Avoid visiting forests without local guidance.
Respect tribal communities and customs.
