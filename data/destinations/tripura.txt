Destination: Tripura
Tripura is a northeastern Indian state known for palaces, temples, forests, and cultural heritage.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Agartala:
The capital city known for palaces and temples.

Ujjayanta Palace:
A historic royal palace and museum.

Neermahal:
A beautiful lake palace surrounded by water.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Mui borok
Chakhwi
Wahan mosdeng
Bamboo shoot dishes
Transportation:

Maharaja Bir Bikram Airport in Agartala is the main gateway.
Local transport includes buses, taxis, and auto-rickshaws.

Travel tips:

Carry comfortable clothing.
Explore local handicrafts.
Respect tribal culture and traditions.
