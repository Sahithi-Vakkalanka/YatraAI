Destination: Arunachal Pradesh
Arunachal Pradesh is a northeastern Indian state known for mountains, monasteries, forests, and natural beauty.

Best time to visit:
October to April.

Popular destinations:

Tawang:
Famous for monasteries, snow-covered mountains, and beautiful landscapes.

Ziro Valley:
Known for rice fields, tribal culture, and music festivals.

Bomdila:
A scenic hill town with monasteries and Himalayan views.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Thukpa
Momos
Zan
Bamboo shoot dishes
Transportation:

Donyi Polo Airport in Itanagar is a major airport.
Local travel includes taxis and shared vehicles due to mountainous terrain.

Travel tips:

Carry warm clothes.
Check permit requirements before visiting border areas.
Road journeys may take longer due to mountain routes.
