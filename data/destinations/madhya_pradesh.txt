Destination: Madhya Pradesh
Madhya Pradesh is a central Indian state known for wildlife, historical monuments, temples, and natural beauty.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Khajuraho:
Famous for ancient temples with detailed sculptures.

Kanha National Park:
Known for forests, wildlife, and tiger safaris.

Ujjain:
A spiritual city famous for temples and religious festivals.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Poha
Bhutte ka kees
Dal bafla
Sabudana khichdi
Transportation:

Devi Ahilya Bai Holkar Airport in Indore and Raja Bhoj Airport in Bhopal are major gateways.
Local transport includes buses, taxis, and rental cars.

Travel tips:

Plan wildlife safaris in advance.
Carry comfortable shoes for monuments and temples.
Avoid summer travel due to high temperatures.
