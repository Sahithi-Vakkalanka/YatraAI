Destination: Maharashtra
Maharashtra is a western Indian state known for beaches, mountains, Bollywood, forts, and cultural heritage.

Best time to visit:
October to February.

Popular destinations:

Mumbai:
India’s financial capital known for beaches, markets, and entertainment.

Lonavala:
A hill station famous for waterfalls, hills, and viewpoints.

Ajanta and Ellora Caves:
Ancient rock-cut caves known for art and architecture.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Vada pav
Misal pav
Puran poli
Modak
Transportation:

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport in Mumbai is the main gateway.
Local transport includes trains, buses, taxis, metro, and rental cars.

Travel tips:

Monsoon is excellent for hill stations.
Book accommodations early in Mumbai.
Respect local festival traditions.
