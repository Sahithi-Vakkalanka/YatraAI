Destination: Uttarakhand
Uttarakhand is a Himalayan state known for mountains, pilgrimage sites, rivers, forests, and adventure activities.

Best time to visit:
March to June and September to November.

Popular destinations:

Rishikesh:
Known for yoga, river rafting, and spiritual tourism.

Nainital:
A hill station famous for lakes and mountain views.

Kedarnath:
A major pilgrimage destination in the Himalayas.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Aloo ke gutke
Kafuli
Bhatt ki churkani
Bal mithai
Transportation:

Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun is the main gateway.
Local transport includes buses, taxis, and shared vehicles.

Travel tips:

Check weather before mountain trips.
Carry warm clothes and medicines.
Book pilgrimage travel in advance.
