Destination: Haryana
Haryana is a northern Indian state known for ancient heritage, agriculture, folk culture, and historical sites.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Kurukshetra:
A historic city associated with the Mahabharata and important pilgrimage sites.

Gurugram:
A modern city known for business hubs, shopping malls, and entertainment.

Morni Hills:
A scenic hill area known for lakes, forests, and nature trails.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Bajra roti
Kadhi
Churma
Kachri ki sabzi
Transportation:

Indira Gandhi International Airport in Delhi serves as the main gateway.
Local transport includes buses, taxis, metro services, and rental cars.

Travel tips:

Winter is best for sightseeing.
Carry comfortable clothing for rural and heritage visits.
Respect local traditions and customs.
