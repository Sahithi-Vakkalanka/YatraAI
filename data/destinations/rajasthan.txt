Destination: Rajasthan
Rajasthan is a western Indian state known for deserts, palaces, forts, royal history, and colorful culture.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Jaipur:
Known as the "Pink City" for forts, palaces, and heritage buildings.

Udaipur:
Famous for lakes, palaces, and romantic landscapes.

Jaisalmer:
Known for desert safaris, golden forts, and sand dunes.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Dal baati churma
Laal maas
Gatte ki sabzi
Kachori
Transportation:

Jaipur International Airport is the main gateway.
Local transport includes trains, buses, taxis, and rental cars.

Travel tips:

Carry sun protection during desert trips.
Wear comfortable footwear for forts.
Book desert camps in advance during peak season.
