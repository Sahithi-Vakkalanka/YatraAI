Destination: Sikkim
Sikkim is a Himalayan state known for mountains, monasteries, lakes, and natural beauty.

Best time to visit:
March to June and October to December.

Popular destinations:

Gangtok:
The capital city known for monasteries, viewpoints, and mountain scenery.

Tsomgo Lake:
A high-altitude lake surrounded by snow-covered mountains.

Pelling:
Known for views of Mount Kanchenjunga and peaceful landscapes.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Momos
Thukpa
Phagshapa
Gundruk soup
Transportation:

Pakyong Airport is the main airport.
Bagdogra Airport in West Bengal is also commonly used.
Local transport includes taxis and shared vehicles.

Travel tips:

Carry warm clothes.
Check permits for restricted areas.
Travel carefully on mountain roads.
