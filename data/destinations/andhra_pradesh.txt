Destination: Andhra Pradesh
Andhra Pradesh is a state in southeastern India known for temples, beaches, rich culture, and spicy cuisine.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Visakhapatnam:
A coastal city famous for beaches, hills, and scenic viewpoints.

Tirupati:
Known for the famous Tirumala Venkateswara Temple and spiritual tourism.

Araku Valley:
A hill station known for coffee plantations, waterfalls, and tribal culture.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Pulihora
Andhra chicken curry
Gongura pachadi
Pesarattu
Transportation:

Visakhapatnam International Airport and Vijayawada Airport are major gateways.
Local transport includes buses, taxis, auto-rickshaws, and rental vehicles.

Travel tips:

Carry sun protection during summer.
Try local spicy cuisine carefully if you are not used to it.
Respect temple customs and traditions.
