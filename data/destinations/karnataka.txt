Destination: Karnataka
Karnataka is a southern Indian state known for ancient temples, beaches, forests, technology, and diverse landscapes.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Bengaluru:
Known as India's technology hub with gardens, museums, and modern attractions.

Mysuru:
Famous for the Mysore Palace, heritage, and festivals.

Hampi:
A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for ancient ruins and temples.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Bisi bele bath
Mysore masala dosa
Ragi mudde
Mangalorean fish curry
Transportation:

Kempegowda International Airport in Bengaluru is the main gateway.
Local transport includes metro, buses, taxis, and rental vehicles.

Travel tips:

Visit heritage sites early to avoid crowds.
Carry light cotton clothes in summer.
Try regional cuisines from different parts of the state.
