Destination: Goa
Goa is a western Indian state famous for beaches, nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and seafood.

Best time to visit:
November to February.

Popular destinations:

Baga Beach:
Popular for water sports, restaurants, and nightlife.

Old Goa:
Known for historic churches and colonial architecture.

Dudhsagar Falls:
A scenic waterfall surrounded by forests.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Goan fish curry
Prawn balchão
Bebinca
Pork vindaloo
Transportation:

Goa International Airport is the main gateway.
Local transport includes taxis, buses, rental scooters, and rental cars.

Travel tips:

Book accommodation early during peak season.
Carry sunscreen and beach essentials.
Respect beach safety rules.
