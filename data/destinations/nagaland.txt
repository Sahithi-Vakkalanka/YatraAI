Destination: Nagaland
Nagaland is a northeastern Indian state known for tribal heritage, festivals, hills, and natural beauty.

Best time to visit:
October to May.

Popular destinations:

Kohima:
Known for the Hornbill Festival, war memorial, and scenic hills.

Dzukou Valley:
Famous for trekking trails, flowers, and beautiful landscapes.

Mokokchung:
Known for tribal culture and traditional villages.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Smoked pork
Bamboo shoot curry
Axone dishes
Galho
Transportation:

Dimapur Airport is the main gateway.
Local transport includes taxis and shared vehicles.

Travel tips:

Carry warm clothes during winter.
Book accommodation early during festivals.
Respect tribal customs and traditions.
