Destination: Mizoram
Mizoram is a northeastern Indian state known for hills, forests, waterfalls, and rich tribal culture.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Aizawl:
The capital city known for scenic hills, churches, and local markets.

Phawngpui (Blue Mountain):
The highest peak in Mizoram, famous for wildlife and panoramic views.

Vantawng Falls:
A beautiful waterfall surrounded by green forests.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Bai
Sawhchiar
Vawksa rep
Bamboo shoot dishes
Transportation:

Lengpui Airport near Aizawl is the main gateway.
Local transport includes taxis, buses, and shared vehicles.

Travel tips:

Carry comfortable clothes for hill travel.
Check entry requirements before visiting.
Respect local traditions and community rules.
