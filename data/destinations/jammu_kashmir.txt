Here is the 29-state version of India (before the 2019 reorganization). The additional state to include is Jammu and Kashmir.

Destination: Jammu and Kashmir
Jammu and Kashmir was a northern Indian state known for Himalayan landscapes, valleys, lakes, gardens, and rich cultural heritage. (Since 2019, it has been reorganized into the Union Territories of Jammu and Kashmir and Ladakh.)

Best time to visit:
March to October.

Popular destinations:

Srinagar:
Famous for Dal Lake, houseboats, Mughal gardens, and scenic beauty.

Gulmarg:
Known for snow activities, skiing, meadows, and mountain views.

Pahalgam:
A beautiful valley known for rivers, forests, trekking, and peaceful landscapes.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Rogan josh
Wazwan
Yakhni
Kahwa
Transportation:

Sheikh Ul-Alam International Airport in Srinagar is the main gateway.
Local transport includes taxis, buses, rental cars, and tourist vehicles.

Travel tips:

Carry warm clothes, especially during winter.
Check weather and travel conditions before visiting.
Respect local culture and traditions.
Book accommodation early during peak tourist seasons.
