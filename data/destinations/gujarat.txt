Destination: Gujarat
Gujarat is a western Indian state known for heritage sites, wildlife, festivals, and vibrant culture.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Rann of Kutch:
Famous for the white salt desert and Rann Utsav festival.

Gir National Park:
Known as the home of Asiatic lions.

Ahmedabad:
A historic city known for architecture, food, and culture.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Dhokla
Thepla
Khandvi
Undhiyu
Transportation:

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport in Ahmedabad is a major gateway.
Local transport includes buses, taxis, trains, and rental vehicles.

Travel tips:

Visit the Rann of Kutch during the festival season.
Carry sun protection during daytime travel.
Try local vegetarian cuisine.
