Destination: Manipur
Manipur is a northeastern Indian state known for lakes, hills, dance, and unique cultural traditions.

Best time to visit:
October to April.

Popular destinations:

Imphal:
The capital city known for markets, museums, and historical sites.

Loktak Lake:
Famous for floating islands and scenic beauty.

Keibul Lamjao National Park:
Known as the home of the endangered Sangai deer.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Eromba
Chamthong
Singju
Kangshoi
Transportation:

Bir Tikendrajit International Airport in Imphal is the main gateway.
Local transport includes taxis, buses, and shared vehicles.

Travel tips:

Carry light warm clothing during winter.
Check local travel guidelines before visiting remote areas.
Respect local customs and traditions.
