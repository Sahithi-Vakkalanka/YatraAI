Destination: Kerala

Kerala is a state in southern India known as God's Own Country.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Munnar:
A hill station famous for tea plantations, waterfalls,
and trekking.

Alleppey:
Known for backwater cruises and houseboats.

Wayanad:
Known for forests, wildlife, waterfalls,
and adventure activities.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:
- Appam with stew
- Kerala Sadya
- Puttu
- Fish curry

Transportation:

Kochi International Airport is the main gateway.
Local transport includes buses, taxis, and rental cars.

Travel tips:

Carry light cotton clothes.
Monsoon season receives heavy rainfall.
Respect local culture and traditions.
