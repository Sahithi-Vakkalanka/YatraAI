Destination: West Bengal
West Bengal is an eastern Indian state known for culture, literature, mountains, beaches, and historical landmarks.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Kolkata:
Known for colonial architecture, art, literature, and festivals.

Darjeeling:
Famous for tea gardens, toy train, and Himalayan views.

Sundarbans:
Known for mangrove forests and Bengal tigers.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Macher jhol
Rasgulla
Mishti doi
Shorshe ilish
Transportation:

Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport in Kolkata is the main gateway.
Local transport includes metro, buses, trains, taxis, and rental cars.

Travel tips:

Visit during Durga Puja for cultural experiences.
Carry warm clothes for hill areas.
Respect wildlife rules in forest regions.
