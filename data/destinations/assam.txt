Destination: Assam
Assam is a northeastern Indian state famous for tea gardens, wildlife, rivers, and vibrant festivals.

Best time to visit:
October to April.

Popular destinations:

Kaziranga National Park:
Known for one-horned rhinoceroses and wildlife safaris.

Guwahati:
A major city known for temples and the Brahmaputra River.

Majuli:
A river island famous for culture and traditional arts.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Assam fish curry
Khar
Pitha
Masor tenga
Transportation:

Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport in Guwahati is the main gateway.
Local transport includes buses, ferries, taxis, and rental cars.

Travel tips:

Visit during winter for comfortable weather.
Carry mosquito protection.
Respect local traditions and wildlife rules.
