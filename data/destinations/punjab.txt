Destination: Punjab
Punjab is a northern Indian state known for Sikh heritage, farming, festivals, music, and food.

Best time to visit:
October to March.

Popular destinations:

Amritsar:
Famous for the Golden Temple and cultural attractions.

Chandigarh:
Known for modern architecture, gardens, and planned city design.

Patiala:
Famous for royal heritage, palaces, and traditional culture.

Cuisine:

Popular dishes:

Butter chicken
Amritsari kulcha
Sarson da saag with makki di roti
Lassi
Transportation:

Sri Guru Ram Dass Jee International Airport in Amritsar and Chandigarh Airport are major gateways.
Local transport includes buses, taxis, auto-rickshaws, and rental cars.

Travel tips:

Cover your head while visiting Sikh religious places.
Winter is ideal for sightseeing.
Enjoy local food in moderation as many dishes are rich.
